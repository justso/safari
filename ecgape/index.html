ECGape Safari 5 extension

This extension injects some styles into the Aprimo pages (CSS, see ingredients below).

It is simple, client-side tweaking — just your browser, self-updating, and easily disabled.
It was made to help us overcome some of the design defects that still exist, such as:

  • Hiding the dangerous Close and Cancel buttons in the Project Workflow Management pages.
  • Fixing the logo on the Main Window tab to the correct proportions and white background.
  • Moves the calendar day-picker icon to be near to the entry field.
