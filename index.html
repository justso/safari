Yahpaz Safari 5 extension

This extension injects some scripts and styles into local pages.

It is simple, client-side tweaking — just your browser, self-updating, and easily disabled.
It was made to help me test and morph various things, such as:

  • Hiding the ... pages.
  • Fixing the ... background.
  • Moving the ... to the entry field.
